The Workhorse: gistCmodule

The reader should be familiar with many of the functions in gistCmodule from the chapter ``Two-Dimensional Plotting Functions'' on page . Most of the gistCmodule functions discussed there, and many of the helper functions, are pretty close to literal translations of the equivalent functions in Gist, the main difference being the superstructure built on top of them in order to handle PyObjects. In addition to the plotting functions, a number of functions in gistCmodule are essential for maintenance of that module, and are discussed here. We also discuss briefly a few other functions which are not literal translations of Gist functions.
